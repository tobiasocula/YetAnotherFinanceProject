SHARPE BASED

-costs: heavily dependent on cash allocation (expected)
-stressses: lower with lower gamma, lower with higher stress coef
-max drawdown: slightly better when gamma lower, stress coef lower
-mean return: better when higher stress coef, worse when cash param is lower
-sortino: slightly better when stress coef low, slightly better when cash param = 0.5
-sharpe: same as sortino
-cvar: only dependent on cash allocation, slightly better when cash alloc is higher
-var: same

MOMENTUM BASED WITH CVAR

-cash allocation: almost constant
-costs: slightly better when lambda higher
-stress values: slightly better when lambda higher
-max drawdown: mostly depends on m-parameter allocations: slightly better when using parameters [0.25 0.5  0.25]
-var: almost constant as var
-cvar: same as var
-sharpe: slightly better when using m-parameters [0.33, 0.33, 0.33]
-sortino: same as sharpe
-mean return: slightly better when lambda higher
